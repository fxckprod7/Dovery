Product Details

What is Dovery?

Dovery is an AI-powered grocery delivery app
designed to streamline your entire food
planning experience. From personalized meal
suggestions to smart shopping and seamless
delivery, it’s the ultimate food assistant in your
pocket.

Our Innovation:

Personalized meal generation based on your preferences, budget, and health goals.

Real-time grocery inventory management and one-click order placement.

Integration with local stores for faster, more sustainable delivery.

Our Edge:

Unlike standard delivery apps or static recipe
platforms, Dovery combines health tracking,
automation, and personalized planning — all in
one sleek app.

Revenue Model Snapshot:

We earn through a mix of order commissions,
partnerships with local supermarkets, and premium
subscriptions with exclusive perks.

Target Users:

Time-strapped workers, busy parents, students
on a budget, and fitness lovers who want
smarter, healthier eating without the hassle.
